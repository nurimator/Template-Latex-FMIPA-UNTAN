\chapter*{PREFACE}
\begin{spacing}{1.5}
The preface contains a brief description of the purpose of writing the thesis and acknowledgments. The length of the preface should ideally not exceed one page. Acknowledgments should only be addressed to parties who have supported and assisted in the writing of the thesis. The order of acknowledgments should be arranged based on the level of contribution to the completion of the thesis. Examples of the writing format are as follows:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Name of the influential party followed by a description of the influence provided to the author and their contribution to the completion of the thesis.
    \item Acknowledgments should be written based on the order of the party's influence in the writing of this thesis.
\end{enumerate}
\end{spacing}
